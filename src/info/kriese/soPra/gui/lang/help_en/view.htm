Reset 3D-Visualization

This button switches the 3D-visualization back to the original state. The coordinate system will be centered and the axes will be aligned correctly.


 

Show 3D-Fullscreen-Window

Here you can switch the 3D-visualization to a fullscreen window separated from the main program window. This gives you a more detailed view on the LOP.


 

Show Dual LOP

Shows the Dual LOP of the actual edited LOP.


 

Show Primal LOP

Shows the primal LOP of the actual edited LOP.
